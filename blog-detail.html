More and more Business owners are asking the questions: What is a Virtual Office and how can my business benefit? Business is changing. Advances in technology are helping to make life easier around the globe, and businesses are no exception. Business owners and industry leaders are turning to the digital world to increase profits and run businesses more easily. Plus they are improving their work-life balance. Sound too good to be true? It’s easy to do with one simple service: the virtual office. Prior to 2020, the concept of running a business remotely already took the industry by storm. In 2020, running your business remotely became a necessity and the norm. You would be hard-pressed to find a businessperson today who was unaware of the lasting impact of this towards digitized, flexible practices. Indeed, more and more companies are choosing to run their business virtually, but have you ever wondered how they do it, and why? They do it with a carefully chosen virtual office package that allows them to work from anywhere in the world while they maximize their efficiency, cut their overhead costs, and expand their hiring pools. Interested? Here’s what you need to know:

A virtual office can come with a variety of features that maximize your efficiency and boost your business credibility. Depending on your provider, a virtual office can also provide features such as virtual mailbox services, local or toll-free phone numbers, live receptionists or answering services, access to meeting spaces, and more! At Starthub, choose from three great packages: a Premium Business Mailbox, a Virtual Office, and a Virtual Office + Access. A Premium Business Mailbox includes a premium virtual address and full mail and package handling services. A Virtual Office offers the same basic features, plus a live receptionist to handle all of your incoming calls. A Virtual Office + Access plan gives you all of those services, plus day passes to your premium office location so you can come in and work for the day!
